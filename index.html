About Tiny Melon


Cherish your little stories

We are tinymelon and we know being a family is the most awesome, scary, hilarious, anxious, joyous and downright exhausting thing you'll ever do. Fraught moments stretch like eternity, while precious years go by in a heartbeat. Becoming a parent means you're never alone, but sometimes you feel exactly that. We're right with you, because at Tinymelon, we're a family too. So while you're bringing up humans, we're bringing up ideas. And, like you, putting our absolute all into it. We believe all families deserve the best we can give. Quality; naturally. Affordability; definitely. Compromise; not on our watch. We challenge ourselves to make your life easier. To swap niggles for giggles, from pregnancy to primary school and beyond. So that you can win this crazy-fun family thing!

At Tinymelon we bring products that are made with hand by artisans from Vietnam .
