Our Tiny Little Story

We are tinymelon and we know having a child is the most amazing, frightening, hilarious, anxious, joyous and exhausting thing you'll ever do. We understand you, at tiymelon we are a family too! From the first scans we start imagining a life where our babies are feeling secure fully surrounded by our love and care.
Tinymelon was born from the desire to see more children play and make friends with toys which are thoroughly tested against British safety standards. Many handmade toys on the UK market have not been tested which means a constant hazard for the safety of our children. We made it a mission to develop UKCA/CE certified handmade baby products that are safe, affordable and beautiful and it was not an easy task.
We know that babies experience real-life objects through chewing so any plastic parts such as eyes or nose have been replaced with yarn while still retaining their beautiful design. Anything that gets in touch with your baby’s skin should be gentle in order to prevent skin irritation, that is why these toys have been made with non-toxic, certified fabric. We would love it if you could join us in our journey to contributing back to society. Because we believe no child should be without love and care our mission is to create a circle of giving.
By purchasing these handmade toys you contribute towards donations to the Hy Vong Home for Disabled Children located in the Sui Cat district of Vietnam where farming families cannot afford their specialized care. Please visit their website
